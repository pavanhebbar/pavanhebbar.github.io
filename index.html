What I've done

Numerical Simulation of Collisionless Shocks

Implementing Particle-in-Cell method using warp code to simulate collisionless shocks. Used the results to analyse the variation of shock parameters with changing plasma paramters. Going to publish a paper

Pratham -IITB Student Satellite Team

Working in the mechanical subsystem. Involved in simulation and analysis of satelite response to structural and thermal loads. Proposed the SNAP model to start the staellite instruments as soon as it is ejected from the launch vehicle. Published paper that was selected for International Aerospace Congress 2014, Toronto, Canada.

Using OH Megamasers to verify Galaxy Evolution theories

Studied various kinds of astronomical masers and their properties. Studied interference and synthesis imagin ing. Aimed at using the variation of number density and mass of OH megamasers with redshift to verify galaxy evolution theories.

Gamma Ray detection through Cherenkov Radiation

Studied various concepts related to detection of gamma rays. Wrote programs to diffrentiate between cherenkov showers due to gamma rays from cosmic rays. Studied Crab nebula and MRK421 in detail.
